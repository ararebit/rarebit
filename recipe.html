Shrimp with Green Sauce

Serious Eats
http://www.seriouseats.com/recipes/2012/02/the-secret-ingredient-parsley-shrimp-with-green-sauce.html

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 3-ounce bunch parsley
  • 3 scallions
  • 1/2 jalapeno
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • Sea salt
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 pound large peeled and deveined shrimp
